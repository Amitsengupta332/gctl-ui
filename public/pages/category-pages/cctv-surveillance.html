FAQ

CCTV Surveillance Questions

Which CCTV camera is best for outdoor use? ›

For outdoor use, bullet cameras, IP cameras and PTZ cameras with weatherproof protection such as IP66 or IP67 are commonly used.

What is the difference between NVR and DVR? ›

NVR is usually used with IP cameras, while DVR is commonly used with analog or HD CCTV cameras. Both are used for video recording and storage.

Can I view CCTV cameras from mobile? ›

Yes, most modern CCTV systems support mobile app viewing, remote monitoring and real-time alerts through internet connection.
