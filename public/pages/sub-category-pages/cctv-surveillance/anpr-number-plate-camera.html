FAQ

ANPR Camera Questions

What is an ANPR camera? ›

ANPR stands for Automatic Number Plate Recognition. It captures and reads vehicle license plates for parking, gate access and security monitoring.

Where are ANPR cameras used? ›

ANPR cameras are commonly used in parking areas, toll gates, building entrances, factories, commercial properties and vehicle checkpoints.

Can ANPR cameras work at night? ›

Yes, ANPR cameras with IR support can capture number plates in low-light and night-time conditions.
