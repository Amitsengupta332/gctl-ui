FAQ

AI Camera Questions

What is an AI camera? ›

An AI camera is a security camera with intelligent video analytics features such as human detection, vehicle detection, face recognition, people counting and smart alerts.

Where are AI cameras used? ›

AI cameras are used in offices, factories, warehouses, shopping malls, parking areas, entrances, schools, hospitals and other security-sensitive locations.

Can AI cameras reduce false alarms? ›

Yes, AI cameras can reduce false alarms by identifying specific objects such as people or vehicles instead of triggering alerts for every movement.
