FAQ

HD CCTV Camera Questions

Is HD CCTV camera good for outdoor security? ›

Yes, outdoor HD CCTV cameras with weatherproof protection are suitable for entrances, parking areas, building sides and perimeter monitoring.

Which is better, bullet or dome HD CCTV camera? ›

Bullet cameras are commonly used for outdoor long-distance monitoring. Dome cameras are commonly used for indoor, office and ceiling-mounted installations.

Can HD CCTV cameras record at night? ›

Yes, most HD CCTV cameras include IR night vision support for low-light and night-time monitoring.
