Frequently Asked Questions

Which CCTV category should I choose? +

If you need basic monitoring, HD CCTV cameras are suitable. For advanced network-based systems, IP cameras and NVR solutions are better. For smart detection, choose AI cameras or video analytics.

Do you provide installation support? +

Yes, professional installation and project support can be provided depending on the product category and project requirements.

Can these products be used for commercial buildings? +

Yes, these product categories are suitable for offices, factories, shopping malls, hospitals, hotels, apartment buildings, warehouses, and other commercial spaces.

Can I customize the security system based on my project? +

Yes, security and automation systems can be customized based on site size, camera quantity, access points, recording duration, monitoring requirements, and budget.
